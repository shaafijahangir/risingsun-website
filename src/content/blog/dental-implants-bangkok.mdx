## What the Process Involves

Dental implants take 3–6 months in total. Most of that time is healing, and it happens whether you are in Bangkok or back home.

**Stage 1: Consultation and extraction (if needed)**
X-rays, 3D CT scan, bone density assessment, and treatment planning. Extraction, if needed, is done at the same appointment or the following day. Typically included in the implant package.

**Stage 2: Implant placement**
The titanium post is placed under local anaesthetic. 45–90 minutes. You leave the same day. Mild discomfort for 3–5 days.

**Stage 3: Osseointegration**
The implant fuses with the bone over 3–6 months. You do not need to be in Bangkok for this.

**Stage 4: Crown placement**
One appointment. Many patients combine this with a return trip, or have the crown placed by their home dentist using Bangkok specifications and abutment components.

The practical approach for most people: Trip 1 to Bangkok for consultation and implant placement (3–5 days). Wait 3–6 months at home. Trip 2 for crown placement, or coordinate with your home dentist.

## Same-Trip Options

For patients who cannot make two trips, immediate loading implants (crown placed on the same day) are available for suitable candidates. Bone density must meet specific criteria. Worth raising at your consultation.

All-on-4 and All-on-6 full-arch procedures are available at both hospitals above and can be completed within 5–7 days including healing time.

## What to Bring From Home

Your Bangkok dentist will take their own X-rays and CT scan. Bringing existing records and recent X-rays speeds up the consultation and can reveal relevant history: previous root canals, bone grafts, failed implants. If you have had implants fail before, bring those records. Bumrungrad handles implant failure cases regularly.

## Recovery

Implant placement recovery is mild. Most patients are comfortable for sightseeing and restaurant meals within 24–48 hours. Avoid hard foods for the first week. Avoid smoking: it significantly affects osseointegration success rates.

You can fly home 24–48 hours after placement with no clinical risk. If you had a complex extraction or bone graft alongside, wait 3–5 days.
